"DIY: Create Vibrant Natural Colors at Home!"

The article provides a guide on how to create natural, homemade colors using various ingredients like red sandalwood powder, turmeric, spinach leaves, blue hibiscus flowers, and beetroot, all mixed with flour and water. The process involves making a paste, letting it dry, and then crushing it into a fine powder.

Color Ingredients Procedure
Red Red Sandalwood Powder, Flour
Mix red sandalwood powder with flour in equal proportions. Add a little water to make a thick paste. Let it dry and then crush it to make a fine powder.
Yellow Turmeric Powder, Gram Flour
Mix turmeric powder and gram flour in equal proportions. Add a little water to make a thick paste. Let it dry and then crush it to make a fine powder.
Green Spinach Leaves, Flour
Grind spinach leaves to make a fine paste. Mix it with flour and add a little water. Let it dry and then crush it to make a fine powder.
Blue Blue Hibiscus Flowers, Flour
Crush blue hibiscus flowers to make a fine paste. Mix it with flour and add a little water. Let it dry and then crush it to make a fine powder.
Pink Beetroot, Flour
Grind beetroot to make a fine paste. Mix it with flour and add a little water. Let it dry and then crush it to make a fine powder.
