2024: The Year in Review

A look back at the defining moments, trends, and breakthroughs of 2024. From pivotal elections and economic shifts to cultural milestones and technological leaps, this is an interactive summary of a year of significant change and progress.

Global Landscape

The year was marked by major electoral contests and significant geopolitical developments around the world. This section highlights some of the key events that shaped the international political scene.

🗳️ U.S. Presidential Election

A highly anticipated election saw record voter turnout. The results signaled a shift in the political landscape, with key battleground states playing a decisive role.

🇪🇺 European Parliament Election

Elections across the EU in June resulted in a notable shift to the right, impacting the bloc's legislative agenda on climate, migration, and economic policy for the next five years.

🇮🇳 India General Election

The world's largest democratic exercise concluded with Prime Minister Narendra Modi's coalition securing another term, though with a reduced majority, leading to new political dynamics.

🕊️ Global Peace Initiatives

Several high-level diplomatic talks aimed at resolving ongoing conflicts took place, with mixed results. International focus remained on efforts in Eastern Europe and the Middle East.

🌍 Climate Policy Summits

Nations convened to assess progress on the Paris Agreement. Debates centered on funding for developing nations and accelerating the transition away from fossil fuels amidst energy security concerns.

🇬🇧 UK General Election

The United Kingdom held a general election that resulted in a significant change in government, with the Labour Party winning a large majority, ending 14 years of Conservative rule.

Innovation & Discovery

Technological and scientific advancements continued to accelerate, promising to reshape industries and improve lives. This section covers the most impactful breakthroughs of the year.

🤖 Generative AI Evolution

New, more powerful large language models (LLMs) were released, capable of more complex reasoning, code generation, and multi-modal understanding. AI integration into consumer and enterprise products became mainstream.

🚀 Advances in Space Exploration

The Artemis program progressed with successful uncrewed tests, paving the way for future lunar missions. Private companies expanded satellite constellations, enhancing global connectivity.

🧬 CRISPR Gene Editing Therapies

Following initial approvals, the first patients were successfully treated for genetic disorders using CRISPR-based therapies, marking a new era in personalized medicine.
