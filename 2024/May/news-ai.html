May 2024 AI and GenAI News- Summary of May GenAI update

Amazon has launched Q, an AI-powered assistant designed to enhance productivity for businesses and developers.
Q Developer facilitates advanced code generation, testing, debugging, reasoning, and provides planning agents.
Q Business integrates with company data systems, allowing users to access and analyze information, summarize trends, and respond to queries efficiently.
The Q Apps feature enables non-technical users to create custom AI applications using natural language, streamlining interactions with enterprise data.

Ukraine is breaking new ground by deploying Victoria Shi, an AI-powered spokesperson, for their Ministry of Foreign Affairs. Victoria assists human diplomats by delivering updates and information on consular affairs.

While human staff crafts the content of Victoria's messages, the AI avatar delivers them visually. To ensure authenticity and combat potential manipulation, each statement comes with a QR code linking to the official text version on the ministry's website. This innovative approach marks a global first in using AI avatars for official government communications.
X, formerly known as Twitter, has launched a new feature called Stories, which provides AI-generated summaries of trending news within the app.
This feature, available to X Premium subscribers, offers quick news updates in the Explore section, reducing the need for users to scroll extensively.
Powered by Grok AI, the summaries are based on data from X to ensure relevance and accuracy.
Users are cautioned about potential inaccuracies in the AI-generated summaries and advised to check facts independently.
Microsoft is creating a new AI model called MAI-1, led by Mustafa Suleyman. This model aims to compete with leaders like OpenAI and Google.
MAI-1, with 500 billion parameters, is set to handle complex tasks and improve on previous Microsoft AI models.
It's being trained using data from OpenAI's GPT-4 and the public internet, enhancing its knowledge and capabilities.

Meta Boosts Advertising with AI and Verification:

  • AI-Powered Ad Creation: Meta Platforms is rolling out new ad tools that leverage AI to generate ad copy and images based on existing content. This will help advertisers create variations and optimize campaigns.
  • Global AI Expansion: These AI features are planned for global rollout by the end of 2024, with ongoing analysis of past campaigns to further refine them.
  • Meta Verified Goes Global: Meta is expanding its Meta Verified service to more countries, allowing more small businesses to benefit from increased credibility and user engagement.

    p>Google I/O 2024 was a whirlwind of announcements, with a heavy focus on artificial intelligence (AI) advancements. Here's a quick rundown of some of the key updates:

    Search and Assistant Upgrades

    • Gemini AI: Google's search engine got a major upgrade with an improved version of Gemini. This means more informative search results and the ability to use text prompts to get help with tasks like creating meal plans.
    • AI Teammate: A new feature called "AI Teammate" is being integrated into Google Workspace. This AI assistant can create searchable summaries of your work from emails, messages, and documents.
    • Multimodal Search: Google is testing new search features that go beyond text. One example is the ability to upload a video of a product and ask the search engine to identify it or troubleshoot a problem.

    Generative AI

    • Imagen 3: This upgraded image generation tool boasts better text understanding and the ability to create images with finer details.
    • Veo: A brand new app from DeepMind that allows users to create high-definition videos using text prompts. Veo even understands cinematic terms, so you can ask it to generate a time-lapse video with an aerial shot.

    Other Announcements

    • Android 15: The next iteration of Android promises better battery life and the ability to cast any cast-enabled app directly to your car's display (with Rivian being the first automaker on board).
    • Wear OS 5: This update to Google's wearable platform focuses on improved efficiency, offering 20% more battery life and new health metrics for runners.
    • Firebase Genkit: Developers can now leverage this new tool to create AI-powered applications more easily.

    Apple to Supercharge Siri with Next-Gen AI


    Get ready for a smarter Siri! Apple is gearing up for a major upgrade to its virtual assistant, powered by cutting-edge artificial intelligence. This revamp will allow users to control individual features within apps using just their voice.

    This marks a significant leap for Siri, as it will now be able to manage a wider range of tasks within your favorite apps. To achieve this, Apple is undertaking a major overhaul of Siri's underlying software, incorporating powerful large language models - a key technology behind impressive AI advancements.
