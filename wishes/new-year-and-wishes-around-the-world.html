Cultural Deep Dive

Beyond the Gregorian calendar's January 1st, many cultures celebrate the new year at different times, based on lunar, solar, or lunisolar calendars. This section offers a deeper look into some of these vibrant celebrations. Click on any celebration below to expand the card and learn about its unique history, traditions, and significance.

Celebration Calendar Types

The timing of New Year celebrations is determined by different calendar systems. This chart provides a visual breakdown of the calendar types for the cultural festivities highlighted in this application. Hover over a segment to see which system—like Gregorian, Lunar, or Lunisolar—governs each tradition, offering a glimpse into the diverse ways cultures mark the passage of time.
