Cancer in Chinese Zodiac
The Chinese Zodiac does not have a Cancer sign as found in Western astrology. Instead, Chinese astrology is based on a 12-year cycle with each year represented by an animal. However, for those born under the Cancer sun sign in Western astrology (June 21 - July 22), the closest equivalent in the Chinese Zodiac could be the sign of the Sheep (also known as the Goat or Ram), which is known for its gentle and compassionate nature.
Myths and Folktales In Western astrology, Cancer is represented by the crab and is associated with the myth of the Lernaean Hydra, one of the Twelve Labors of Hercules. In this myth, the crab is sent by the goddess Hera to distract Hercules during his fight with the Hydra, but it is crushed underfoot. Hera, in recognition of its service, places the crab among the stars as the constellation Cancer.
Strengths Cancers are known for their nurturing and protective nature. They are highly intuitive, empathetic, and compassionate, making them excellent caregivers and friends. Their loyalty and ability to empathize with others' pain and suffering are among their greatest strengths.
Weaknesses Their sensitivity can sometimes lead to moodiness and a tendency to take things too personally. Cancers can also be quite clingy and insecure, which may lead to challenges in their relationships if not managed well.
Motivations Cancers are motivated by the need for emotional security and a strong sense of home and family. They strive to create a safe and comfortable environment for themselves and their loved ones. Their actions are often driven by their heart and their desire to care for others.
Compatibility with Other Signs Cancers are most compatible with the water signs Scorpio and Pisces, as they share a deep emotional understanding. They also find common ground with earth signs Taurus and Virgo, appreciating their stability and grounded nature. Challenges may arise with fire and air signs, who may be too impulsive and detached for the sensitive Cancer.
Romantic Relationships In romantic relationships, Cancers are caring and affectionate partners who crave deep emotional connections. They are most compatible with partners who understand their need for intimacy and who can provide the security they seek. They are loyal and committed, often putting their partner's needs before their own, and they expect the same level of commitment in return.
Friendship Bonds As friends, Cancers are supportive and compassionate. They form bonds by being attentive and genuinely caring about the well-being of their friends. They are great listeners and often the shoulder to cry on. However, they need friends who respect their emotional space and who can provide the reassurance and loyalty they value.
