Astrological Aspects: Unity, Harmony, Tension, Flow

```html
Aspect Angle Keywords Description
Conjunction 0° Unity, Blend, Power
A conjunction occurs when two planets are in the same place in the zodiac (0° apart). This aspect symbolizes a blending of the energies of the planets involved. It can be a powerful and harmonious influence if the planets share similar natures, but it can also indicate tension if the planets have conflicting qualities. Conjunctions can signify new beginnings or powerful focal points of energy in a person's life.
Sextile 60° Harmony, Opportunity, Talent
The sextile is an aspect formed when planets are about 60° apart. It is considered a harmonious aspect that opens the door to communication, exchange of ideas, and the opportunity for growth. Sextiles often bring about situations where learning and adapting are favored, and they can indicate natural talents that, when nurtured, lead to success.
Square 90° Tension, Challenge, Growth
Squares occur when planets are 90° apart and indicate a relationship of tension and conflict, leading to growth and development. This aspect can manifest as internal or external struggles that force a person to overcome obstacles and challenges. While squares can be difficult, they are also the driving force behind achievement and character building.
Trine 120° Flow, Ease, Talent
Trines are formed when planets are approximately 120° apart. This is a harmonious aspect that suggests a flow of energy between the planets involved. Trines often indicate natural abilities or ease in the areas of life they touch. They can bring about periods of good luck, ease, and well-being, but there's a risk of complacency due to the lack of challenge.
Opposition 180° Polarity, Awareness, Balance
An opposition is when planets are directly across from each other on the zodiac wheel, 180° apart. This aspect represents a tug of war between opposing forces or energies. It can bring about a need for balance and often manifests as relationships or external circumstances that challenge one's sense of self. Oppositions can lead to greater self-awareness as individuals work to find harmony between the two extremes.
Quincunx (Inconjunct) 150° Adjustment, Transition, Karmic
The quincunx, or inconjunct, is an aspect formed when planets are about 150° apart. It is considered a minor aspect that can create a sense of unease or discomfort as the energies of the planets involved do not easily blend. This aspect often requires adjustments and can indicate karmic lessons or a need for change in certain areas of life. It's a call to develop new perspectives or lifestyles.
Semi-Sextile 30° Opportunity, Potential, Mild
The semi-sextile is a minor aspect that occurs when planets are 30° apart. It can indicate a mild interaction between the energies of the planets that can lead to opportunities if one is willing to take initiative. While not as powerful as a sextile, the semi-sextile can still offer moments where small adjustments or decisions can lead to significant improvements over time.
Sesquiquadrate 135° Tension, Effort, Irritation
The sesquiquadrate, also known as the sesquisquare, is an aspect of 135° between planets. It is a minor aspect that can create friction and irritation, similar to a square. This aspect suggests a need for adjustment and can bring about situations that require effort to resolve. It's often associated with minor annoyances or unresolved issues that need attention.
```
