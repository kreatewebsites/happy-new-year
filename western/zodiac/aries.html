Aries Zodiac Sign Details
Date Range March 21 - April 19
Symbol The Ram
Element Fire
Ruling Planet Mars
Myths and Folk Tales The most prominent myth associated with Aries is that of the Golden Ram, which is linked to the Greek tale of the hero Jason and his quest for the Golden Fleece. The ram was sent by the god Hermes to rescue Phrixus and Helle, children of the Boeotian king Athamas, from their stepmother's wrath. Phrixus was saved by the ram, and in gratitude, he sacrificed it to the gods and presented its golden fleece to King Aeetes. The fleece was later sought by Jason and the Argonauts.
Strengths Aries individuals are known for their courage, confidence, and enthusiasm. They are natural leaders, often taking initiative and inspiring others. Their determination and competitive spirit can drive them to achieve great things.
Weaknesses Their weaknesses can include impatience, impulsiveness, and a tendency to be quick-tempered. Aries may also struggle with being self-centered and having difficulty finishing tasks they consider mundane or boring.
Motivations Aries are motivated by a desire for new experiences and personal triumphs. They thrive on challenges and often seek out adventures that allow them to demonstrate their bravery and leadership skills.
Compatibility with Other Signs Aries is most compatible with other Fire signs (Leo and Sagittarius) as they share the same energy and zest for life. They also get along well with Air signs (Gemini, Libra, and Aquarius) who can match their dynamic nature and appreciate their straightforwardness.
Romantic Relationships In romantic relationships, Aries are passionate and direct. They are attracted to partners who are equally strong and independent. Aries can be very affectionate and loyal, but they need a partner who can handle their intensity and give them the space they sometimes require.
Friendship Bonds As friends, Aries are fun-loving and always ready for an adventure. They form bonds with those who appreciate their boldness and are willing to embark on spontaneous journeys. They are supportive and encouraging friends, often pushing their companions to take risks and try new things.
