Libra in Chinese Zodiac
The Chinese Zodiac, known as Shengxiao, is based on a twelve-year cycle, with each year related to an animal sign. However, Libra is not an animal sign in the Chinese Zodiac. Instead, Libra is a sign from the Western Zodiac, which is based on the solar calendar and relates to the position of the sun at the time of one's birth. The Western Zodiac sign of Libra is symbolized by the Scales and covers those born between September 23 and October 22. In the context of the Chinese Zodiac, a person born under the sign of Libra would be associated with the animal sign of the year in which they were born.
Myths and Folktales There are no specific myths or folktales associated with the Libra sign in the context of the Chinese Zodiac. However, in Western astrology, Libra is often associated with the Greek goddess of justice, Themis, or her daughter Dike, who was known for her fairness and was depicted holding the scales of justice.
Strengths and Weaknesses Strengths: Diplomatic, fair-minded, social, cooperative, gracious, and charming.
Weaknesses: Indecisive, avoids confrontations, will carry a grudge, self-pity.
Motivations Libras are motivated by a strong sense of justice and a desire for harmony and balance in their lives and relationships. They strive for fairness in all aspects and are often motivated by the pursuit of partnership and shared experiences.
Compatibility with Other Signs Libras are most compatible with fellow air signs Gemini and Aquarius, as they share the same intellectual and social approach to life. They also have a high compatibility with fire signs Aries, Leo, and Sagittarius, which can bring energy and passion to Libra's balanced nature.
Romantic Relationships In romantic relationships, Libras are loving and devoted partners. They thrive in a balanced and harmonious relationship and often go to great lengths to maintain peace and equilibrium. They are most compatible with signs that appreciate their need for partnership and can offer them the affection and admiration they crave.
Friendship Bonds As friends, Libras are sociable, generous, and thoughtful. They are excellent listeners and give sound, balanced advice. They form strong bonds with those who respect their need for harmony and who enjoy socializing and engaging in intellectual discussions.
