Location Description
Goa
Goa is one of the most popular places to celebrate New Year in India. The entire state is lit up with parties and events. Beach parties, cruises, and fireworks are some of the highlights. The most popular places in Goa to celebrate New Year are Anjuna, Calangute, and Baga beaches.
Mumbai
Mumbai, the city that never sleeps, is another great place to celebrate New Year. The city is known for its high-energy parties. From five-star hotels to open-air venues, there are plenty of options to choose from. The most popular places in Mumbai to celebrate New Year are Marine Drive and Juhu Beach.
Bangalore
Bangalore, the Silicon Valley of India, is known for its lavish New Year celebrations. The city offers a mix of traditional and modern celebrations. The most popular places in Bangalore to celebrate New Year are MG Road and Brigade Road.
Delhi
Delhi, the capital city of India, offers a variety of options for New Year celebrations. From luxurious parties in five-star hotels to concerts and events in open-air venues, there is something for everyone. The most popular places in Delhi to celebrate New Year are Connaught Place and Hauz Khas Village.
Kolkata
Kolkata, the cultural capital of India, celebrates New Year with a lot of enthusiasm. The city offers a variety of options for celebrations, from parties in clubs and hotels to cultural programs and events. The most popular places in Kolkata to celebrate New Year are Park Street and Salt Lake.
