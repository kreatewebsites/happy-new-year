Event Description
Grand Park New Year's Eve LA
Grand Park New Year's Eve LA is one of the biggest free New Year's Eve celebrations in California. It's a family-friendly event that features live music, food vendors, and a stunning light projection on the side of the City Hall. The event culminates in a countdown to midnight and a spectacular fireworks display.
San Francisco's New Year's Eve Fireworks
San Francisco's New Year's Eve Fireworks is a must-see event. The fireworks are launched from barges located 1,000 feet out in the bay, just south of the Ferry Building. The best viewing spots are along the Embarcadero, from Mission Street to Folsom Street. The show starts at the stroke of midnight.
New Year's Eve Yacht Party - San Diego
This is a unique way to ring in the New Year. The New Year's Eve Yacht Party in San Diego offers a three-level luxury yacht party with open bar all night, plenty of food, and DJs spinning top hits. The yacht sails around the San Diego Harbor, providing stunning views of the city skyline.
Orange County New Year's Eve Block Party
The Orange County New Year's Eve Block Party is a massive event that features multiple stages with live music and DJ performances, a variety of food trucks, and a midnight countdown with a fireworks show. The event takes place at the OC Fair & Event Center in Costa Mesa.
New Year's Eve at Disneyland
Disneyland in Anaheim is a magical place to celebrate New Year's Eve. The park is adorned with festive decorations, and there are special events and entertainment throughout the day, including parades, live music, and character meet and greets. At midnight, there's a spectacular fireworks display over Sleeping Beauty Castle.
