5 Catchy New Year's Eve Party Themes

Theme Description
Glitter and Gold
For a glamorous New Year's Eve, consider a Glitter and Gold theme. Decorate your space with gold balloons, glittery tablecloths, and gold tableware. You can also add a touch of sparkle to your food and drinks with edible glitter.
Black and White Ball
A Black and White Ball theme is a classic and elegant choice for New Year's Eve. Ask your guests to dress in black and white, and decorate your space with black and white balloons, streamers, and tablecloths. You can also serve black and white food and drinks, like black and white cookies and black and white martinis.
Roaring Twenties
Bring back the glamour and excitement of the Roaring Twenties with a 1920s-themed New Year's Eve party. Decorate your space with art deco elements, feathers, and pearls. Serve classic 1920s cocktails, like gin fizzes and sidecars, and play jazz music to set the mood.
Winter Wonderland
Create a magical Winter Wonderland with a white and silver color scheme. Decorate your space with white balloons, silver tinsel, and twinkling fairy lights. You can also serve winter-themed food and drinks, like white chocolate desserts and hot chocolate.
Disco Dance Party
Get your guests moving with a Disco Dance Party theme. Decorate your space with disco balls, neon lights, and colorful tablecloths. Play classic disco hits and serve fun, retro food and drinks, like cheese fondue and tequila sunrises.
