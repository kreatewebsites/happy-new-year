Party Themes for Every Occasion

Party Type Theme Description
Family Party Classic New Year's Eve
Decorate your home with classic New Year's Eve decorations like balloons, streamers, and confetti. Serve traditional New Year's foods and drinks, and have a countdown to midnight.
Family Party Movie Marathon
Choose a series of movies to watch together as a family. You could choose a theme like "Best Movies of the Year" or "Family Favorites". Serve popcorn and other movie snacks.
Friends Party Costume Party
Ask your friends to come dressed in costumes. You could choose a theme like "Decades" or "Favorite Movie Characters". Have a contest for the best costume.
Friends Party Game Night
Set up different game stations around your home. You could have board games, video games, and card games. Serve snacks and drinks that are easy to eat while playing games.
Professional Colleague Party Black and White Ball
Ask your colleagues to dress in black and white. Decorate your home in black and white as well. Serve elegant foods and drinks, and have a dance floor for dancing.
Professional Colleague Party Wine and Cheese Tasting
Set up a wine and cheese tasting station. You could have different types of wine and cheese for your colleagues to try. You could also have a sommelier come in to talk about the different wines and cheeses.
