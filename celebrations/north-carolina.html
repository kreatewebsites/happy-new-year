"Ring in the New Year with These Festive Events!"

Event Description
First Night Raleigh
First Night Raleigh is a popular New Year's Eve celebration in downtown Raleigh. This family-friendly event features live music, performances, and art installations throughout the city. The night culminates with the famous Raleigh Acorn Drop and fireworks at midnight.
New Year's Eve at Biltmore
For a more upscale celebration, consider spending New Year's Eve at the Biltmore Estate in Asheville. The estate offers a variety of celebration options, including a gala at the Biltmore House, a party at the Antler Hill Barn, or a dinner at the Dining Room at the Inn on Biltmore Estate.
CLT New Year's Eve
Charlotte's official New Year's Eve celebration, CLT New Year's Eve, is held at Romare Bearden Park. The event features food, music, and a countdown to midnight with a fireworks display. It's a fun and festive way to ring in the New Year in the Queen City.
Island of Lights New Year's Eve Countdown
For a beachside New Year's Eve celebration, head to Carolina Beach for the Island of Lights New Year's Eve Countdown. The event features a giant lighted beach ball drop, fireworks, and live entertainment. It's a unique and festive way to ring in the New Year.
Thirsty Monk New Year's Eve
If you're a craft beer enthusiast, consider spending New Year's Eve at the Thirsty Monk in Asheville. The brewery hosts a New Year's Eve party with special beer releases, a toast at midnight, and plenty of good cheer.
