Right time to buy item on sale

Things on sale in January

Furniture. In Feburary new furniture models are released. January has best sales for furniture.

What is on sale in Feburary

Winter coats, Premium choclates, Electronics equipment such as TV, Audio, Video games. In Feburary winter is near end, so winter clothing goes on sale. Retailers are getting ready to put spring clothing on store. Also after Valentine day - premium choclates are on sale.
In US -Apr 15 is tax filing date. Tax software goes on sale in Feb.
Feburary is also best time to buy a boat

March
Ski gear. If you like Ski - best time to buy Ski Gear is March. Ofcourse you will be able to use it next year.
People travel in summer. Companies released new model of luggage bag, suitcase in summer. Marh is best month to buy existing model.

April
Used Cars. During this time car deals get vehicles at auction. April is best month to buy used car.
Also April is National Car Care month in USA. This is best time to get car service, buy parts for car, backup camera , tires etc
Cookware and Home improvement items goes on sale in April.

May
Mattress. The best sale on mattress come on Memorial day.
It is also a good time to buy summer clothes as summer is approaching and store start displaying summer clothes.
As wedding season is near and summer is approaching - many retailers discount kitchenware, picnic, grilling supplies.

June
Health club give discount on membership as summer approaches. As more people prefer spend time on outdoor activities, deals on gym membership are easy to found.
Cinemas also offer deal on movie ticket to bring people inside cinema
Cruise price and deal appear in this month.
You can find more laptop, PC deals as June is slower month. More students buy these in July/Aug with back to school event.

July
Father day is in June. July 4 is big weekend. After Father day and July 4 - you will see big sale on Men's items such as Tools, DIY. You will also see sale on Jewelry item in July as between July-Nov there is no occassion for gift giving, no major holiday for shopping.

August
In August Back-to-school is major event and deal comes on school bags, kids clothes, laptops, stationary. Sooner you buy better it is.
Also as summer is approaching end - items related to summer such as grills, lawn mower, luggage items, camping come on sale.

September
September is end of summer. Bicyles, camping , outdoor activities items come on sale.
Car companies start brining new models, so previous models are available on sale in Sep.
With start of school less people travel on vacation between Sep and thanksgiving, this is great time to get flight ticket, holiday packages discount.
Cameras are on sale too as holiday time is over and companies bring new models during ThanksGiving.
Leftovers from Back To School event come on sale - laptops, PC. (as new model are launches during thanks giving and Christmas time)

Octobor
Oct is getting ready for Thanks Giving.
Companies that are launching new phone model, smart watch model - lower the price of existing model.
Car price continue to be lower in Oct

November
Nov is known for Blak Friday and ThanksGiving sale. Nowadays retailers have started sale early.
You should buy electronics on sale. Also if you find good deal - buy and keep it. If you find a lower deal on Thanks Giving you can exchange/return earlier items. Costco allows it for 30 days. Various other retailer allow you to return for 14 days
Begining of Nov - you will also see sale on Halloween leftovers - candy and gift items.

December
Day and week after Christmas is biggest sale. In Europe Dec 26 is known as Boxing day and famous for sale. However nowdays retaiers can predict how sale is going and start sale on weekend in Dec.
Example Macy does number of sale in Dec. Similarly many electronics seller do a good sale in Dec
