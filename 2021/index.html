COVID-19 Vaccines Multiple vaccines were developed and distributed globally to combat the COVID-19 pandemic.

George Floyd Verdict Derek Chauvin was found guilty of the murder of George Floyd, a Black man whose death sparked nationwide protests against police brutality and racial injustice.
Space Exploration Multiple space missions were launched, including NASA's Perseverance rover landing on Mars and SpaceX's Crew Dragon successfully transporting astronauts to the International Space Station.
Climate Change Action Several countries, including the United States, pledged to take action to combat climate change and reduce carbon emissions.
Women's Rights Several countries, including Argentina and Mexico, legalized abortion and took steps to protect women's rights.
COVID-19 Relief Multiple countries passed legislation to provide financial relief to individuals and businesses affected by the COVID-19 pandemic.
Technology Advancements Advancements in technology were made, including the development of new AI and machine learning tools.
Social Justice Movements Several social justice movements gained momentum, including the Black Lives Matter movement and the fight for LGBTQ+ rights.
Sports Achievements Several athletes achieved historic accomplishments, including Naomi Osaka winning her fourth Grand Slam title and Tom Brady winning his seventh Super Bowl.
